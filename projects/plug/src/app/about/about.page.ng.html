Who We Are

Our Mission

We are on a mission to democratize education by making peer support accessible, affordable, and effective for every university student. We believe that the best way to learn is often from someone who has just walked the path before you.

The Story

Academic Plug started as a simple dorm room idea to help friends pass difficult calculus exams. What began as a small WhatsApp group for sharing notes has grown into a platform connecting thousands of students across campuses to share knowledge, find tutors, and succeed together.
